Tribute to the forg

- - BACKGROUND - -

"The Forg" is an iconic piece of internet culture that originated on Reddit. It showcases a delightful drawing of a frog with only two legs and a comically misspelled caption reading "forg" instead of "frog." This whimsical creation quickly gained widespread popularity and turned into a beloved meme, spreading like wildfire across Reddit and numerous other social media platforms.

- - MORE INFO - -

The Forg originated from a post on a social media platform called Reddit back on September 22nd, 2020.
